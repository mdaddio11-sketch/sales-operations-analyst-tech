# HPE Artificial Intelligence Strategy

Source: https://www.hpe.com/us/en/solutions/artificial-intelligence.html
Date: 2026-05-04

HPE is positioning itself as a leading AI infrastructure provider for enterprises and governments. Key AI initiatives include:

- HPE Private Cloud AI: Turnkey AI factory solution built with NVIDIA
- HPE Cray Supercomputing: Leading provider of AI supercomputers for national labs
- HPE Machine Learning Development Environment (MLDE)
- Partnership with NVIDIA for AI Computing by HPE portfolio
- AI-native networking through Juniper Mist platform

HPE has won major AI supercomputing contracts including systems for Oak Ridge National Laboratory, Los Alamos National Laboratory, and others.
