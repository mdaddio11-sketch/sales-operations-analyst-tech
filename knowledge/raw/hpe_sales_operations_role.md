# HPE Business Analyst Sales Operations Role

Source: https://www.hpe.com/us/en/careers.html
Date: 2026-05-04

The Business Analyst, Sales Operations role at HPE focuses on enabling the sales organization through data analysis, process improvement, and cross-functional collaboration.

Key responsibilities:
- Analyze sales pipeline data to identify trends and opportunities
- Build and maintain dashboards for sales leadership
- Support CRM data quality and hygiene initiatives
- Partner with Sales, Finance, and Marketing on forecasting
- Develop and document sales processes and playbooks
- Drive adoption of sales tools and technologies

Required skills: SQL, Excel, Salesforce CRM, data visualization, stakeholder communication
