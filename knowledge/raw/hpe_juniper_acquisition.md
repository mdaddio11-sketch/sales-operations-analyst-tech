# HPE Acquisition of Juniper Networks

Source: https://www.hpe.com/us/en/newsroom/press-release/2025/07/hewlett-packard-enterprise-closes-acquisition-of-juniper-networks.html
Date: 2025-07-02

HPE completed its acquisition of Juniper Networks on July 2, 2025. The acquisition doubles the size of HPE networking business and creates an industry-leading AI-native networking portfolio.

Key details:
- Transaction valued at approximately  billion
- Combines HPE Aruba Networking with Juniper Mist AI platform
- Creates comprehensive portfolio from campus to data center to WAN
- Accelerates HPE shift to higher-margin software and services revenue
- CEO Antonio Neri called it a transformational combination
